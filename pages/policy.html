Privacy Policy

Current as of July 2024

Your privacy is important to us at Oursupport. We respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect from you across our website and services.

Last modified: July 2024

I. Introduction / II. Overview / III. Data collections and uses / IV. Choice and transparency / V. Updates to this notice

I. Introduction.

When you utilize OurSupport, you entrust us with your personal information. We are dedicated to maintaining that trust. This begins with assisting you in understanding our privacy policies. This notice outlines the personal information we gather, how we use and share it, and the options you have concerning this data.

II. Overview.

A. Scope.

This notice is applicable to users of OurSupport services globally, which includes users of the OurSupport app, website, features, or other services associated with the OurSupport app.

This notice outlines how OurSupport and its affiliates gather and utilize personal data. This notice is relevant to all users of our app, website, features, or other services globally, unless a separate privacy notice covers them. This notice specifically pertains to:
• Requestor (Customer): Individuals or companies who request or receive IT support, including those who receive IT support requested by another person.
• Field Engineers (Service Representative): Individuals who offer IT support services to requestors either individually or through partner service companies.
This notice also regulates other collections of personal data in relation to OurSupport services. For instance, we may gather the contact information of individuals who use accounts owned by OurSupport for Business customers or of owners or employees of OurSupport partners. We may also gather the personal data of those who initiate but do not complete applications to be Service Representatives or customers, those who provide services or are subcontracted by other companies through the OurSupport mobile app, or in relation to our mapping technology and features. All those subject to this notice are referred to as ‘users’ in this notice.

Our data practices are subject to the laws applicable in the locations in which we operate. This implies that we engage in the practices outlined in this notice in a specific country or region only if the laws of those places allow it. Please contact us using the contact us on our website www.oursupport.co or through the addresses below if you have any queries regarding our practices in a specific country or region.

B. Data Controller and Transfer.

The entity responsible for the collection of personal data in relation to the use of OurSupport services is Tsolnetworks Connect Europe, S.A, also known as OurSupport. We may transfer data to countries other than where our users reside or use OurSupport services. This is done to honor our agreements with users, like our Terms of Use, or based on users’ prior consent, adequacy decisions for the respective countries, or other transfer mechanisms as permitted by the law.

For any questions, comments, or complaints about OurSupport's data practices, please send them to queries@oursupport.co. Alternatively, you can use the form on our website www.oursupport.co to submit your query.

III. Data Collections and Uses.

A. The Data We Collect.

OurSupport gathers the following:

• User-provided data during interactions with OurSupport, such as when creating an account.
• Data generated during the utilization of our services, like location, ticket activity tasks, app usage, and ticket-related data given by the requestor.
The subsequent data is gathered by OurSupport or on its behalf:

1. User-Provided Data. This encompasses:

• User Profile: We gather data when users establish or update their OurSupport accounts. This could include their name, email, phone number, country, city, password, and profile picture. We do not request or gather payment or banking details (including associated payment verification information), driver’s license, and other government identification documents (which may reveal document numbers as well as birth date, gender, and photo) within the OurSupport mobile application.
• We might use the photos submitted by users to confirm their identities, such as through facial recognition technologies. For additional information, please refer to the section titled “How we use personal data.”
• Background Check and Identity Verification: We utilize all accessible social media and online systems to conduct a background check and verify the identity of Service Representatives.
• User Content: We gather the information users provide when they reach out to or use the OurSupport app. This could include feedback, photographs, or other recordings collected by users.

2. Data Generated During Service Use. This encompasses:

• Location Data (Service Representatives): OurSupport gathers this data when the OurSupport app is operational, and the “TRAVEL” task has been initiated by the Service Representative. The app can be running in the foreground (app open and on-screen) or background (app open but not on-screen) of their mobile device.
• Transaction Information: We gather transaction details related to the use of our services, including the type of services requested or provided, order details, delivery information, date and time the service was provided, and distance traveled.
• Usage Data: We gather data about how users interact with our services. This includes data such as access dates and times, app features or pages viewed, app crashes and other system activity, type of browser, and third- party sites or services used before interacting with our services. In some cases, we gather this data through cookies, pixels, tags, and similar tracking technologies that create and maintain unique identifiers.
• Device Data: We may gather data about the devices used to access our services, including the hardware models, device IP address, operating systems and versions, software, preferred languages, unique device identifiers, advertising identifiers, serial numbers, device motion data, and mobile network data. We use the app store’s standard collection platform for this.
• Communications Data: We enable users to communicate with each other and OurSupport through OurSupport’s mobile apps and website. For example, we enable Customers and Service Representatives to update comments, report documents such as user intervention reports, goods delivered documents, and activity tasks (without disclosing their telephone numbers and personal information to each other). To provide this service, OurSupport stores the above-mentioned data regarding all ticket activity, including the date and time of the updates, comments, activities. OurSupport may also use this data for customer support services (including to resolve disputes between users), for safety and security purposes, to improve our products and services, and for analytics.

3. Data from Other Sources. This encompasses:
• User feedback, such as ratings, feedback, or compliments on our website or on the release mobile app store.
• Users or others providing information in connection with claims or disputes.
• Publicly available sources.
• Marketing service providers.
OurSupport may combine the data collected from these sources with other data in its possession.

B. How We Use Personal Data.

OurSupport gathers and utilizes data to provide reliable and user-friendly IT support services. We also use the collected data for:

• Improving the precision, safety, and security of our services.
• Delivering customer support.
• Conducting research and development.
• Facilitating communication between the Provider and the customer.
• Legal proceedings.
OurSupport does not sell or share user personal data with third parties for their direct marketing without users’ consent.

OurSupport uses the collected data for various purposes including:

1. Providing Services and Features: OurSupport uses the collected data to deliver, personalize, maintain, and enhance our products and services. This includes using the data to:
• Establish and update users’ accounts.
• Confirm Service Representatives’ identity, background history, and eligibility to work.
• Propose, process, or facilitate reports for our services.
• Monitor and share the progress of a ticket request.
• Carry out internal operations necessary to deliver our services, including troubleshooting software bugs and operational issues; conducting data analysis, testing, and research; and monitoring and analyzing usage and activity trends.

2. Safety and Security: We use personal data to help ensure the safety, security, and integrity of our services and users. This includes:

• Screening Service Representatives before allowing their use of our services and at subsequent intervals, including through reviews of background checks, where permitted by law, to help prevent the use of our services by unsafe individuals.
• Using data from devices to help identify inaccurate travel claims on each ticket request.
• Using device, location, profile, usage, and other data to prevent, detect, and combat fraud or unsafe activities.

3. Customer Support: OurSupport uses the collected information to deliver customer support, including to:

• Direct questions to the appropriate customer support person.
• Investigate and address user concerns.
• Monitor and enhance our customer support responses and processes.

4. Research and Development: We may use the collected data for testing, research, analysis, product development, and machine learning to improve the user experience. This helps us to improve and enhance the accuracy, efficiency, safety, and security of our services, improve our ability to prevent the use of our services for illegal or improper purposes, develop new features and products, and facilitate insurance and finance solutions in connection with our services.

5. Enabling Offline Communications Between Users. For instance, a Customer may update a comment, note, or add a document to a ticket request.

6. Marketing: OurSupport may use the collected data to promote our services to our users. This includes sending users communications about OurSupport services, features, studies, surveys, news, updates, and events. We may also send communications to our users about products and services offered by OurSupport partners. We may use the collected data to personalize the ticket allocation to Service Representatives, including based on user location and user preferences and settings. We may also send users communications regarding elections, ballots, referenda, and other political and notice processes that relate to our services. For instance, OurSupport has notified some users by email public holidays relating to the availability of OurSupport’s services in certain locations.

7. Non-marketing Communications: OurSupport may use the collected data to generate and provide users with ticket receipts; inform them of changes to our terms, services, or policies; or send other communications that aren’t for the purpose of marketing the services or products of OurSupport or its partners.

8. Legal Proceedings and Requirements: We may use the personal data we collect to investigate or address claims or disputes relating to the use of OurSupport’s services, or as otherwise allowed by applicable law, or as requested by regulators, government entities, and official inquiries.

9. Automated Ticket Routing: We use personal data to make automated decisions relating to routing a ticket/request to a Service Representative. This includes:

• Using Service Representative country and city information to identify the location of the Service Representative. • Using the Service Representative’s previous recorded location information to confirm the location of the Service Representative.

C. Cookies and Third-party Technologies.

OurSupport, along with its partners, utilizes cookies and other similar identification technologies on our website for the purposes outlined in this notice.

Cookies are tiny text files that websites, apps, online media, and advertisements store on browsers or devices. OurSupport employs cookies and comparable technologies for objectives such as:

• Verifying users
• Retaining user preferences and settings
• Assessing the popularity of content
• Implementing and gauging the success of advertising campaigns
• Examining site traffic and trends, and broadly comprehending the online behaviors and interests of individuals who engage with our services.

We might also permit others to deliver audience measurement and analytics services for us, to disseminate advertisements on our behalf across the Internet, and to monitor and report on the effectiveness of those advertisements. These entities might employ cookies, web beacons, SDKs, and other technologies to recognize the devices used by visitors to our websites, as well as when they visit other online sites and services.

D. Data Sharing and Disclosure.

Some products, services, and features of OurSupport necessitate the sharing of data with other users or as per a user's request. We might also share data with our customers, affiliates, subsidiaries, and partners for legal purposes or in relation to reporting, audits, claims, or disputes. The data we collect at OurSupport may be shared as follows:

1. With customers
This encompasses sharing:
• The first name, contact information, and location (country and city) of the Service Representative.
• We might also share ratings, feedback, or other information as required by law, with the customer, partner, or Service Representative.

2. At the User’s Request: This involves sharing data with:
• Other individuals at the user's request. For instance, we might share a user's ETA and location with a customer or another Service Representative.

3. With the General Public:
Queries or comments from users submitted via public forms or social media pages may be visible to the public, including any personal data included in the queries or comments submitted by a user.

4. With OurSupport Subsidiaries and Affiliates:
We share data with our subsidiaries and affiliates to assist us in providing our services or conducting data processing on our behalf.

5. For Legal Reasons or in the Event of a Dispute:
OurSupport may share users’ personal data if we believe it’s mandated by applicable law, regulation, operating license or agreement, legal process or governmental request, or where the disclosure is otherwise appropriate due to safety or similar concerns. This includes sharing personal data with law enforcement officials, public health officials, other government authorities, airports (if required by the airport authorities as a condition of operating on airport property), or other third parties as necessary to enforce our Terms of Service, user agreements, or other policies; to protect OurSupport’s rights or property or the rights, safety, or property of others; or in the event of a claim or dispute relating to the use of our services. This also includes sharing personal data with others in connection with, or during negotiations of, any merger, sale of company assets, consolidation or restructuring, financing, or acquisition of all or a portion of our business by or into another company.

6. With Consent:
OurSupport may share a user’s personal data other than as described in this notice if we notify the user and they consent to the sharing.

E. Data Retention and Deletion.

OurSupport retains user data for as long as necessary for the purposes described above. Users may request deletion of their accounts at any time. OurSupport may retain user data after a deletion request due to legal or regulatory requirements or for the reasons stated in this policy. OurSupport retains user data for as long as necessary for the purposes described above. This means that we retain different categories of data for different periods of time depending on the category of user to whom the data relates, the type of data, and the purposes for which we collected the data.

Users may request deletion of their account at any time via email to remove@oursupport.co. Following an account deletion request, OurSupport deletes the user’s account and data, unless they must be retained due to legal or regulatory requirements, for purposes of safety, security, and fraud prevention, or because of an issue relating to the user’s account such as an outstanding credit or an unresolved claim or dispute. Because we are subject to legal and regulatory requirements relating to Service Representatives, this generally means that we retain their account and data for a minimum of 5 years after a deletion request except where retention is necessary for the above reasons.

IV. Choice and Transparency.

OurSupport provides users with the ability to manage and control the data collected by OurSupport, this includes:

• Settings within the app.
• Permissions on the device.
• Pages for ratings within the app.
• Opting out of marketing.

Additionally, OurSupport allows users to request access to their data or copies of it, make changes or updates to their accounts, delete their accounts, or ask OurSupport to limit the processing of their personal data.

A. Privacy Settings.

When the travel task in the OurSupport app is initiated, users are asked to either grant or deny access to their location-sharing preferences.

• Location Data:
The OurSupport app utilizes the location services of the Service Representatives' devices solely during the Travel activity. This is to generate map route details during the travel activity to validate the travel activity, location, time, and distance. Through the Settings menu on their mobile device's operating system, Service Representatives can choose to allow or deny OurSupport access to their device's location data. Without granting location tracking permission to the OurSupport mobile app, users will be unable to log time for the travel activity. All other functions will operate normally even if location permission is turned off.

B. Device Permissions.

Various mobile device platforms such as iOS, Android, etc., have established specific types of device data that cannot be accessed by apps without the consent of the device owner. These platforms have diverse methods for obtaining that consent. For instance, iOS devices alert users when the OurSupport app requests access to certain data types for the first time, providing users with the choice to approve or deny the request. On the other hand, Android devices inform users about the permissions required by the OurSupport app before they start using the app for the first time, and using the app is considered as granting those permissions.

C. User Data Requests.

OurSupport offers a range of methods for users to understand, manage, and voice their queries and comments about how their data is handled by OurSupport. To submit a request, please send an email to OurSupport at queries@oursupport.co and request any of the following:

• Data Access: Users can request an explanation of the data we gather from them and how we utilize it.
• Data Receipt: Users can request a copy of the data that OurSupport collects from them, either with their consent or as necessary to deliver our services.
• Data Modification or Update: Users can alter the name, phone number, email address, and photo linked to their account via the Settings' menu in OurSupport’s apps or online portal. They can also request that OurSupport modify or update their data, especially if they believe the data is incorrect or incomplete.
• Data Deletion: Users can request to delete their account at any time through the Settings > Privacy menus in the OurSupport app, or via OurSupport’s website.
• Objections, Limitations, and Complaints: Users can request that we cease using all or a portion of their personal data, or that we restrict our use of their data. OurSupport may continue to process data following such an objection or request as far as it is required or allowed by law.

V. Updates to This Notice.

From time to time, we may revise this notice. Continued use of our services following an update implies agreement with the revised notice, as far as the law allows. If substantial modifications are made, we will alert users beforehand via the OurSupport app or other methods, such as email. We advise users to regularly check this notice to stay informed about our latest privacy procedures. Following such notification, the use of our services by users from countries outside the European Union will be interpreted as acceptance of the updates, to the extent that the law permits.
