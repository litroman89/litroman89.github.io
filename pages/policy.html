I. Introduction

When you use Oursupport, you trust us with your personal data. We’re committed to keeping that trust. That starts with helping you understand our privacy practices. This notice describes the personal data we collect, how it’s used and shared, and your choices regarding this data.

II. Overview

A. Scope This notice applies to users of Tsolnetworks / Oursupport services anywhere in the world, including users of Tsolnetworks / oursupport app, website, features, or other services related to the Oursupport app.

This notice describes how Oursupport and its affiliates collect and use personal data. This notice applies to all users of our app, website, features or other services anywhere in the world, unless covered by a separate privacy notice. This notice specifically applies to:
• Requestor (Customer): Individuals or companies who request or receive IT Support, including those who receive IT support requested by another individual.
• Field Engineers (Service Representative): individuals who provide IT support services to requestors individually or through partner service companies.
This notice also governs Tsolnetworks / Oursupport other collections of personal data in connection with Oursupport services. For example, we may collect the contact information of individuals who use accounts owned by Oursupport for Business customers or of owners or employees of Oursupport partners. We may also collect the personal data of those who start but do not complete applications to be Service representatives or customer’s, those who provide services or are subcontracted by other companies through the Oursupport mobile app, or in connection with our mapping technology and features. All those subject to this notice are referred to as ‘users’ in this notice.

Our data practices are subject to applicable laws in the places in which we operate. This means that we engage in the practices described in this notice in a particular country or region only if permitted under the laws of those places. Please contact us using the contact us on our website www.tsolnetworks.com or through the addresses below with any questions regarding our practices in a particular country or region.

B. Data controller and transfer

Tsolnetworks Connect Europe, Lda are the data controllers for the personal data collected in connection with use of Oursupport services.
We may also transfer data to countries other than the one where our users live or use oursupport services. We do so in order to fulfill our agreements with users, such as our Terms of Use, or based on users’ prior consent, adequacy decisions for the relevant countries, or other transfer mechanisms as may be available under applicable law.
Questions, comments, and complaints about oursupport data practices can be submitted to queries@oursupport.co. You may also use the form on our website www.tsolnetworks.com to submit a question.

III. Data collections and uses

A. The data we collect

Tsolnetworks / Oursupport collects: • Data provided by users to Oursupport, such as during account creation • Data created during use of our services, such as location, Ticket activity tasks, app usage, and ticket related data provided by the requestor.

The following data is collected by or on behalf of Oursupport:

1- Data provided by users. This includes:
• User profile: We collect data when users create or update their Oursupport accounts. This may include their name, email, phone number, Country, City and password, profile picture. We do not ask or collect , payment or banking information (including related payment verification information), driver’s license and other government identification documents (which may indicate document numbers as well as birth date, gender, and photo) inside of the oursupport mobile application.

• User content: We collect the information users submit when they contact or use the Oursupport app. This may include feedback, photographs or other recordings collected by users.

2- Data created during use of our services. This includes:
• Location data (Iservice representative person): Oursupport collects this data when the Oursupport app is running and the “TRAVEL” task has been started by the service representative, the app can be running in the foreground (app open and on-screen) or background (app open but not on-screen) of their mobile device.
• Location data (Service representatives). We collect precise or approximate location data from Service representatives’ mobile devices if they enable us to do so. Oursupport collects this data when the “travel” task is started and stops collecting location information when the “travel “ task is paused. • Transaction information: We collect transaction information related to the use of our services, including the type of services requested or provided, order details, delivery information, date and time the service was provided and distance traveled.
• Usage data: We collect data about how users interact with our services. This includes data such as access dates and times, app features or pages viewed, app crashes and other system activity, type of browser, and third-party sites or services used before interacting with our services. In some cases, we collect this data through cookies, pixels, tags, and similar tracking technologies that create and maintain unique identifiers.
• Device data: We may collect data about the devices used to access our services, including the hardware models, device IP address, operating systems and versions, software, preferred languages, unique device identifiers, advertising identifiers, serial numbers, device motion data, and mobile network data. we use the app stores standard collection platform for this.
• Communications data: We enable users to communicate with each other and Oursupport through Oursupport´s mobile apps and website. For example, we enable Customers and Service representative persons, to update comments , report documents such as user intervention reports, goods delivered documents and activity tasks ( without disclosing their telephone numbers and personal information to each other). To provide this service, Oursupport stores the above mentioned data regarding all ticket activity, including the date and time of the updates, comments, activities. Oursupport may also use this data for customer support services (including to resolve disputes between users), for safety and security purposes, to improve our products and services, and for analytics.

3- Data from other sources. This includes:
• User feedback, such as ratings, feedback, or compliments on our website or on the release mobile app store.

• Publicly available sources.

Tsolnetworks / Oursupport may combine the data collected from these sources with other data in its possession.

• For customer support.
• For research and development.
• To enable communications between Provider and customer.
• In connection with legal proceedings.

3- Customer support. Oursupport uses the information we collect to provide customer support, including to:

• Direct questions to the appropriate customer support person.
• Investigate and address user concerns.
• Monitor and improve our customer support responses and processes.

4- Research and development. We may use the data we collect for testing, research, analysis, product development, and machine learning to improve the user experience. This helps us to improve and enhance the accuracy , efficiency, safety and security of our services, improve our ability to prevent the use of our services for illegal or improper purposes, develop new features and products, and facilitate insurance and finance solutions in connection with our services.

5- Enabling offline communications between users. For example, a Customer may update a comment, note or add a document to a ticket request.

6- Marketing. Oursupport may use the data we collect to market our services to our users. This includes sending users communications about Oursupport services, features, studies, surveys, news, updates, and events.

We may also send communications to our users about products and services offered by Oursupport partners.
We may use the data we collect to personalize the ticket allocation to Service representatives, including based on user location and user preferences and settings.
We may also send users communications regarding elections, ballots, referenda, and other political and notice processes that relate to our services. For example, Oursupport has notified some users by email public holidays relating to the availability of Ourupport´s services in certain locations .

7- Non-marketing communications.Oursupport may use the data we collect to generate and provide users with ticket receipts; inform them of changes to our terms, services, or policies; or send other communications that aren’t for the purpose of marketing the services or products of Oursupport or its partners.

Oursupport retains user data for as long as necessary for the purposes described above.

Oursupport retains user data for as long as necessary for the purposes described above. This means that we retain different categories of data for different periods of time depending on the category of user to whom the data relates, the type of data, and the purposes for which we collected the data.

IV. Choice and transparency

Oursupport enables users to access and control the data that Oursupport collects, including through:
• In-app settings
• Device permissions
• In-app ratings pages
• Marketing opt-outs

Oursupport also enables users to request access to or copies of their data, changes or updates to their accounts, deletion of their accounts, or that Oursupport restrict its processing of user personal data.

A- Privacy settings

When the Oursupport app travel task is started, the user is promoted to allow or deny access to their location-sharing preferences.

• Location data

Oursupport uses service representatives’ device location services only during the Travel activity within the oursupport app. This is used to plot map route information during the travel activity to ensure that the travel activity, location, time and distance can be justified one.
Service representatives may enable or disallow Oursupport to use location data from their mobile devices through the Settings menu on the operating system of their mobile device. Users will not be able to claim time on the travel activity without granting permission to location tracking to the Oursupport mobile app. All other actives will work as per normal , with the location permission disabled.

B- Device permissions

Most mobile device platforms (iOS, Android, etc.) have defined certain types of device data that apps cannot access without the device owner’s permission, and these platforms have different methods for how that permission can be obtained. iOS devices notify users the first time the Oursupport app requests permission to access certain types of data and gives users the option to grant or refuse permission. Android devices notify users of the permissions that the Oursupport app seeks before their first use of the app, and use of the app constitutes a grant of such permission.

C- User data requests

Oursupport provides users with a variety of ways to learn about, control, and submit questions and comments about Oursupport’s handling of their data.
To make a request, please email Tsolnetworks / Oursupport at queries@oursupport.co and ask for any of the below. • Accessing data: Users can ask for an explanation of the data we collect from them and how we use it.
• Receiving data: Users can ask for a copy of data that Oursupport collects from them with their consent or as necessary to provide our services.
• Changing or updating data: Users can edit the name, phone number, email address and photo associated with their account through the Settings menu in Oursupport’s apps or online portal. They may also ask that Oursupport change or update their data, including if they believe such data is inaccurate or incomplete.
• Deleting data: Users may request deletion of their account at any time through the Settings > Privacy menus in the Oursupport app, or through Oursupport’s website.
• Objections, restrictions, and complaints: Users may request that we stop using all or some of their personal data, or that we limit our use of their data. Oursupport may continue to process data after such objection or request to the extent required or permitted by law.
