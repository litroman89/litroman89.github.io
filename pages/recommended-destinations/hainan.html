Hainan

Sanya Beaches

Popular Sanya beaches, such as Dadonghai and Yalong Bay, offer white sand and clear sea. A great spot for swimming, sunbathing, and water sports.

Yalong Bay

A luxurious resort area with five-star hotels, clean beaches, and crystal-clear water. Enjoy water sports and relax at the spa.

The Guanyin Temple

A significant Buddhist temple complex, famous for its 108-meter-tall Guanyin statue. Enjoy the peaceful atmosphere and beautiful gardens.

Gaoshan Hot Springs

Hot springs in the Gaoshan area offer relaxing treatments and spa experiences in a natural setting.

The South Sea Park

A place to explore marine flora and fauna, including aquariums, exhibits, and educational programs on marine ecology.

The Blue Caves

A location with underground water bodies where you can enjoy swimming and tours through picturesque subterranean landscapes.
