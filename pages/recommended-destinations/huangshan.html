Huangshan
(Yellow Mountains)

Scenic Viewing Platforms

Huangshan has several viewing platforms offering breathtaking views of mountain peaks, clouds, and sunrises/sunsets. Notable platforms include those at "Left Ear Peak" and "Right Ear Peak."

Scenic Views of Clouds

Huangshan is known for its "sea of clouds," where clouds envelop the mountains, creating fantastic vistas. The best spots for cloud viewing are Lotus Peak and the Peak of Death.

Huangshan Hot Springs

The Huangshan Mountains have several hot springs where you can relax and enjoy natural thermal waters. It’s a great place to unwind after long hikes.

The Ancient Town of Tangxi

Located near Huangshan, this historical town offers a glimpse into ancient China and traditional architectural styles.

Tianhu Lake

A small lake in the Huangshan Mountains with picturesque views and a peaceful atmosphere, perfect for relaxation.

Calligraphy or Painting Workshops

The museum showcases a rich collection of ancient ceramics, including famous Sichuan ceramics.
