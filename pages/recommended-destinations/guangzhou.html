Guangzhou

The Guangzhou Tower

One of the tallest towers in the world, standing at 604 meters. Its observation decks offer stunning views of the city, and you can also enjoy attractions such as a glass floor and a Ferris wheel.

Shamian Island

A historic district with colonial architecture, parks, and quiet streets. It’s an excellent place for walks and exploring Guangzhou’s history.

Shamian Pagoda

An ancient pagoda and city symbol. It features traditional Chinese architecture and is a historic monument.

Beijing Road

One of Guangzhou’s most famous shopping streets, where you can find shops, restaurants, and markets.

The Pearl River

A river cruise offers scenic views of the city, especially beautiful in the evening when the city lights up.

Huan Shan Road

Guangzhou’s main business and shopping street, home to office buildings, shops, and restaurants.
