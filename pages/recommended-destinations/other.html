Other Destinations

Dunhuang

Dunhuang is a famous historical city located on the ancient Silk Road. Key attractions: Mogao Caves and Sand Dunes.

Zhangye

Zhangye is famous for its colorful landscapes, especially in the Zhangye Danxia Geopark. This place attracts photographers and nature lovers.

Pingyao

Pingyao is one of the oldest cities in China, renowned for its well-preserved city walls and traditional architecture. A UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Harbin

Harbin is known for its Ice Sculpture Festival and Russian architectural influence. In winter, the city turns into a fairy-tale world of ice.

Nanjing

Nanjing is a city rich in history and cultural heritage. Key attractions: Sun Yat-sen's Mausoleum and the Nanjing City Wall.

Xiamen

Xiamen is a coastal city famous for its mild climate and the charming Gulangyu Island, known for its colonial-style buildings and seafood.
