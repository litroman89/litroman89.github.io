Macau

The Ruins of St. Paul’s Cathedral

One of Macau’s most famous historical sites. The remains of the facade of an old Catholic cathedral, destroyed by fire in 1835, are a city symbol.

Guia Fortress

An ancient fortress with magnificent views of Macau. Built in 1617, it served as a strategic defense point for the city.

Casinos and Entertainment Complexes

Macau is known for its casinos and entertainment complexes, such as The Venetian Macao, Wynn Macau, and MGM Macau. Try your luck at gambling and enjoy luxurious shows and restaurants.

Yacht Cruise

A great way to see the city from a different perspective. Enjoy beautiful views of Macau’s coastline and landmarks.

Coloane Village

A quaint village in Macau known for its colonial architecture and traditional shops. It’s a great place to experience the charm of old Macau.

The Macau Museum

A museum showcasing the history, culture, and art of Macau. It’s a good place to learn about the city’s past and present.
