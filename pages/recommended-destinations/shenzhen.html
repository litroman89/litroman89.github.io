Shenzhen

The "Window of the World" Theme Park

One of Shenzhen’s most famous parks, featuring miniature versions of world-famous landmarks like the Eiffel Tower and the Great Wall of China. It also includes attractions and cultural shows.

Splendid China Folk Village

A theme park that combines miniature replicas of famous Chinese landmarks with displays of traditional Chinese culture and folk art.

Huaqiangbei

A well-known district with high-tech and electronics centers. It’s a great place to buy gadgets and electronics at competitive prices.

Lotus Mountain

A park and mountain area in the center of Shenzhen, where you can climb to the top and enjoy panoramic views of the city.

Dongmen Area

A bustling commercial district where you can find various goods, from clothing to street food. A great place for shopping and experiencing local cuisine.

The Ping An Tower Observation Deck

A high-rise tower with observation decks offering spectacular views of the city and surrounding areas.
