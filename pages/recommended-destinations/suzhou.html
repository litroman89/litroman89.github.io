Suzhou

Suzhou’s Classic Gardens

Suzhou is renowned for its traditional Chinese gardens, which are outstanding examples of garden art. Notable gardens include: Humble Administrator's Garden (Yuan Yuan); Lingering Garden (Ling Yin); Master of Nets Garden (Wangshi Yuan).

Canal Boat Tours in Suzhou

Suzhou is famous for its historic canals, which can be explored by boat. Canal tours offer views of traditional houses, bridges, and ancient architecture.

The Ancient City Wall

Explore well-preserved sections of the ancient city wall that once surrounded the city. It’s a great place for a walk and to gain insight into Suzhou’s past.

The Ancient Market

The Pingjiang Street market offers a variety of Chinese products, souvenirs, and street food. It’s a great place to sample local delicacies and buy traditional goods.

Silk-Making Workshop

Learn about the silk-making process and create your own items under the guidance of a master.

Kung Fu and Traditional Performances

Suzhou is known for its traditional arts, and you can see performances including kung fu and other Chinese theatrical forms.
