Lhasa

The Potala Palace

The famous Tibetan palace and monastery, which was the residence of the Dalai Lama until 1959. The palace is situated on a hill and offers stunning views of the city. It houses treasures, Buddhist relics, and unique works of art.

Jokhang Temple

One of the most sacred temples in Tibet, attracting pilgrims from around the world. The temple has a rich history and is known for its statue of Buddha, considered holy.

Nam Co Lake

Explore well-preserved sections of Nam Co Lake that once surrounded the city. It’s a great place for a walk and to gain insight into lhasa’s past.

The Tibet Museum

A museum featuring exhibits on the history, culture, and art of Tibet. It’s a great place to get an overview of the region.

Tibetan Markets and Shops

Explore local markets like the Choike Market to buy Tibetan jewelry, fabrics, tea, and other traditional items.

Tibetan Cultural Performances

Watch traditional Tibetan dances and musical performances to learn more about local culture and traditions.
