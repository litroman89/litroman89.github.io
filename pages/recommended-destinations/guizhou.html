Guizhou

The Huangguoshu Waterfalls

One of the largest waterfalls in Asia, standing 77.8 meters tall and 101 meters wide. Surrounded by picturesque scenery and tropical forests.

The Ancient City of Jinjiang

A luxurious resort area with five-star hotels, clean beaches, and crystal-clear water. Enjoy water sports and relax at the spa.

Ethnic Minority Villages

A significant Buddhist temple complex, famous for its 108-meter-tall Guanyin statue. Enjoy the peaceful atmosphere and beautiful gardens.

Hot Springs

Guizhou is known for its hot springs, such as those in Anshun, where you can relax and enjoy spa treatments.

The Leiping Village

A village known for its traditional architecture and minority culture. A great place to experience local customs and crafts.

The Zhijin National Park

A natural park with stunning views of mountains, forests, and rivers. A good spot for active recreation and enjoying nature.
